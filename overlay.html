Hide the Safari bar

iPhone Safari can’t remove the URL bar from a normal tab. Add this page to your Home Screen — it opens like an app with no address bar.

  1. Tap Share (□↑) in Safari’s toolbar.
  2. Scroll and tap Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add, then open the new icon — overlay and chat use the full screen.

Android: use the browser menu → “Add to Home screen” or “Install app”. On some devices the Full screen button may hide the bar without installing.
